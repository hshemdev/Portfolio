Instagram User Analytics: Unveiling Insights from a Synthetic Dataset

Introduction

In this project, we delve into the realm of Instagram user analytics, aiming to provide valuable insights to Instagram's product team. By leveraging a synthetic dataset, we have conducted an in-depth analysis to address various marketing and investor-related questions. Our findings shed light on user behavior, engagement patterns, and potential strategies for marketing campaigns. It is important to note that the dataset used in this project is synthetic, designed to simulate real-world Instagram user data.

Marketing Analysis

Loyal User Reward

To reward the most loyal users, we identified the five oldest users on Instagram. These individuals have been actively using the platform for the longest period of time, showcasing their dedication and loyalty to the Instagram community.

Inactive User Engagement

To encourage inactive users to start posting, we identified users who have never shared a single photo on Instagram. By targeting these individuals with promotional emails, Instagram can reignite their interest and motivate them to become active contributors.

Contest Winner Declaration

In a contest where the user with the most likes on a single photo emerges as the winner, we identified the victorious participant and provided their details to the team. This information enables Instagram to acknowledge and reward the contest winner accordingly.

Hashtag Research

To assist a partner brand in maximizing their reach, we identified the top five most frequently used hashtags on the platform. By incorporating these popular hashtags into their posts, the brand can enhance their visibility and engagement with the Instagram community.

Ad Campaign Launch

To optimize the effectiveness of ad campaigns, we determined the best day of the week to launch them. By analyzing the day of the week when most users register on Instagram, we provided insights on when to schedule ad campaigns for maximum user engagement.

Investor Metrics

User Engagement

To gauge user activity and posting frequency, we calculated the average number of posts per user on Instagram. Additionally, we provided the total number of photos on Instagram in relation to the total number of users, offering investors a comprehensive understanding of user engagement on the platform.

Bots & Fake Accounts

To address concerns regarding fake and dummy accounts, we identified users who have liked every single photo on the site. This analysis helps investors assess the presence of bots and fake accounts, ensuring a more accurate evaluation of user engagement and platform authenticity.

Conclusion

Through this project, we have harnessed the power of SQL queries and MySQL Workbench to extract valuable insights from a synthetic Instagram user dataset. Our analysis has provided Instagram's product team with actionable information to enhance marketing strategies, engage inactive users, optimize ad campaigns, and evaluate user engagement. By leveraging these insights, Instagram can foster a thriving community and drive business growth.
